# VAKA Brand Positioning

**Status:** Foundational draft
**Owner:** Brand and Product
**Last reviewed:** 2026-07-04

## Brand name

**VAKA** means **“build” in Shona**.

The name reflects practical progress: building a business, building confidence, building systems, and building durable African enterprises.

## Tagline

> **The Operating System for African Business.**

Use this exact wording and capitalisation unless an approved brand standard defines a context-specific treatment.

## Positioning line

> **Designed in Zimbabwe. Built for Africa.**

Zimbabwe is the first launch market and the source of VAKA’s earliest product insight. It is not a ceiling on the brand. Communications must avoid presenting VAKA as either a generic global product with African decoration or a Zimbabwe-only product with no continental ambition.

## Positioning statement

For African businesses that need greater control over customers, money, stock, and daily operations, VAKA is a trusted business operating system that connects essential workflows and turns business data into clear action. Unlike fragmented tools and generic imported software, VAKA is designed in Zimbabwe around African operating realities and built to localise responsibly across markets.

## Audience

Primary audiences include:

- founders and owner-managers;
- finance and operations leaders;
- sales and customer teams;
- inventory and procurement teams; and
- growing businesses replacing spreadsheets and disconnected tools.

The brand should speak to capable business people. It should never patronise users, over-explain ordinary concepts, or confuse complexity with sophistication.

## Brand promise

VAKA gives African businesses a clearer, safer, and more connected way to run.

We earn this promise through:

- connected operational workflows;
- trustworthy financial and stock records;
- visible permissions and auditability;
- local language and market readiness;
- responsive access across devices;
- practical, permission-aware AI assistance; and
- reliable ownership and export of business data.

## Brand character

VAKA is:

- assured, not arrogant;
- ambitious, not inflated;
- African, not stereotyped;
- modern, not fashionable for its own sake;
- clear, not simplistic;
- warm, not casual about serious work; and
- trustworthy, not bureaucratic.

## Verbal principles

Use direct, concrete business language. Lead with the customer outcome. Prefer evidence to superlatives. Explain technical ideas in terms of control, confidence, time, risk, and business performance.

Avoid:

- vague “revolutionary” or “all-in-one” claims without proof;
- treating Africa as a single homogeneous market;
- deficit narratives about African businesses;
- slang or cultural references that do not travel well;
- unnecessary technical jargon; and
- promising that AI is infallible or autonomous.

## VAKA AI voice

VAKA AI is the executive intelligence layer of the product. It must sound:

- professional;
- calm;
- executive;
- well-spoken;
- concise; and
- business-focused.

It should say what matters, show the basis for its conclusion, identify risk without drama, and end with a useful next step. It must never sound chatty, flippant, breathless, condescending, or falsely certain.

Example:

> Receivables increased 18% this month, mainly from three overdue invoices. Following up with the two largest customers would address 72% of the outstanding balance.

## Language

VAKA must support English, Shona, and Ndebele as first-class product languages. Translations should preserve meaning, dignity, and business clarity rather than mechanically reproducing English sentence structure.

Native or professionally qualified reviewers must approve customer-facing terminology, especially financial, legal, tax, security, and AI content. Language choice must not change a user’s permissions, access to features, or quality of service.

## Current assumptions

- The existing interface currently presents English copy directly in application code.
- The current visual system supports tenant-level brand colours and logos, but a complete VAKA brand book remains separate work.
- The phrase “VAKA OS” may continue as a product identifier, while “VAKA” is the master brand.
- Claims about continental suitability must be supported market by market through research, localisation, compliance review, and product evidence.
